CN2TW

CN2TW is a small too for converting Simplified Chinese HTML files into Traditional Chinese.


CN2TW --all

Convert every html files in the directory from Simplified Chinese into Traditional Chinese.

CN2TW --files file1 file2 file3 ...

Convert specific files from Simplified Chinese into Traditional Chinese.

CN2TW will search for Replacements.txt in the local directory. The format is, first one is Simplified Chinese, the second one is Traditional Chinese.
